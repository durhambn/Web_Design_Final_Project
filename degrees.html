Types of Computer Science Degrees

In the field of computer science, there are many different types of degrees or major you can go for.

There are associates degrees in computer science which typically requires 64 semester hours, while a bachelor's degree in computer science typically requires 128 semester hours.

There are many different types of Bachelor's degrees in Computer Science:

  • Bachelor of Arts (BA) in Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Applied Science (BASc) in Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Computer Security in Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Computing in Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Engineering (BEng) in Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Science in Engineering (Computer Science) â€“ BSE (CS)
  • Bachelor of Mathematics in Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Science (BSc or BS) in Computer Science (BSc CS or BSCS or BSc (Comp))
  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

Not all bachelor's degree programs are the same. It is not uncommon for curriculum and coursework to vary from one school to the next.
