Welcome!

This website contains different information about schools in South Carolina with computer science degrees. When I was looking at colleges, I found it hard to find a complete resouce when researching information about colleges. Therefore, I wanted to make one. The focus of this website is colleges in SC with computer science degrees but it also contains state scholarship information.

The "School Rankings" navigation tab gives a list of all the schools in South Carolina with Computer Science Degrees broken down based on different rankings.

The "Locations" navigation tab gives different locations of the schools.

The "Degree Types" navigation tab gives information about different CS degrees offered by some schools.

The "Scholarships" navigation tab gives information about state scholarships in South Carolina.
