By Brandi Durham

Computer Science Schools in SC Informational Site

Goals:

  1. Inform high school students of colleges or universities in South Carolina that offer computer science degrees and give information on the school.
  2. Be useful to high school seniors.
  3. Have information in one location.
  4. Contain information on the top computer science degrees in South Carolina. This will include information about tuition and scholarships.
