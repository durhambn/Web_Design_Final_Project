Lottery Tuition Assistance Program

Eligible Two-year technical institutions, USC Two-year regional compuses and Spartanburg Methodist College only.

Program was established in 2001, and was created to provide a supplemental resource to SC residents. Eligible full-time students may receive upt o $1,140 per term and eligible part-time students may recieve $95 per credit hour to be used toward the cost of tuition.

In calculating the amount a student is eligible to receive in Lottery Tuition Assistance, all federal grants and the SC Need-based Grant must be awarded first.
