LIFE Scholarship

The LIFE (Legislative Incentive for Future Excellence) Scholarship is a merit-based scholarship program. It may be used towards the cost-of-attendance for up to sight terms.

The LIFE Scholarship Enhancement was established to increase the number of students majoring in math and science fields. Students enrolled in a 2-year intitutions are not eligible to receive the enhancement.

Academic Requirements:

  1. Earn a comulative 3.0 GPA
  2. Score an 1100 on the SAT or 24 on ACT
  3. Rank in the top 30% of the graduating class.
