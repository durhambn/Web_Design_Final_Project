Scholarships for In-State Students

There are many scholarship opportunities for in-state students. Please click on one of the options on the left to learn more about a particular scholarship.

Palmetto Fellows, LIFE, and HOPE are all academic achievement scholarships.

The need-based grants are given based on financial need.

The Lottery Tuition Assistance program is only available to students going to certain colleges.

The Nation Guard College Assistance Program was funded in 2007 and provided incentives for enlisting.
