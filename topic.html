Computer Science Schools in SC Informational Site

My Site Info:

  1. The purpose of my site is to inform high school students of colleges or universities in South Carolina that offer computer science degrees and give information on the school.
  2. I want this site to be useful to high school seniors.
  3. My main intended audience is high school senior looking at potential colleges.
  4. My site is solving the problem of having information in one location.
  5. My site will contain information on the top computer science degrees in South Carolina. This will include information about tuition and scholarships.
